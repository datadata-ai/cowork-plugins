# DATADATA Cowork Plugins

Plugins for [Claude Cowork](https://claude.ai) — built for real users, not developers.
**No terminal. No CLI. No coding. Just add and go.**

---

## How to Install

1. Open **Claude desktop** and go to **Customize**
2. Click **Add marketplace**
3. Paste this:
   ```
   datadata-ai/cowork-plugins
   ```
4. Find the plugin you want and click **Install**

That's it. You're ready.

---

## Available Plugins

### 🔍 Explorium Connector
**B2B intelligence, straight from your Claude chat.**

Powered by [Explorium](https://www.explorium.ai/). Research any company, enrich prospect data, and surface business events — just by asking Claude.

**What you can do:**
- Research a company and get a full intelligence brief
- Find decision-makers and prospects at any company
- Discover business events: funding rounds, new offices, partnerships, product launches
- Uncover events that structured databases miss, using AI-powered web search

**Included skills:**
- `explorium-research` — Full company research in two phases: structured data enrichment + deep web event discovery
- `expansion-upsell-intelligence` — Give Claude your product context, point it at a target company, and get a tailored upsell brief based on real expansion signals

**You'll need:** An Explorium API key — [get one at explorium.ai](https://www.explorium.ai/)
